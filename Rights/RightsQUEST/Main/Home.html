About Us

This project aims to design and develop an online gamified platform that helps children to learn about their rights. It is an interactive gamified platform that is easy to use for children. In this web-based application we will use story telling to convey legal rights and interactive tales that includes decision making. This will also increase legal literacy rate and awareness among children. Access to comprehensive legal knowledge which covers civil, political, social, economic and cultural rights along with regular updates on legal developments. Dividing platform into interactive modules, each focusing on a specific goal and use of gamification elements like virtual money, making learning efficient and enjoyable. By addressing issues of data accuracy, user engagement, and system performance, the project endeavors to enhance the overall legal knowledge. Additionally, the project presents real-life scenarios in a child-friendly manner, allowing children to make decisions and experience the consequences and encourage critical thinking and problem solving.

What are the Rights of children?

Right to Life and Development

Every child has the right to life and to have access to the resources and opportunities that promote their well-being, health, and development. This includes adequate nutrition, shelter, and access to healthcare

Right to Protection

Children must be protected from physical, emotional, and sexual abuse, neglect, and exploitation. Governments and caregivers are responsible for safeguarding children from any form of harm

Right to Education

Children have the right to free, quality education that nurtures their potential and helps them grow intellectually, socially, and emotionally. Primary education must be compulsory and accessible to all

Right to Participation

Children should have the opportunity to express their views and opinions on matters that affect them. Their voices must be considered and respected, taking into account their age and maturity

Right to Health and Well-being

Children are entitled to the best possible health care, safe living conditions, clean water, and proper nutrition, which are necessary for their physical and mental development

Right to Non-discrimination

Every child, regardless of their background, nationality, gender, or disability, has the right to enjoy the same rights and protections. No child should be discriminated against
